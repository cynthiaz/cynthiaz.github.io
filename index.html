ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a UX designer at the IBM Toronto design studio. I studied Industrial Designe with a minor in Psychology at Carleton University. I believe design isn't just about good ideas. It's a process of problem solving, to observe, to think and to develop; learning the why and learning to foresee.

Toolkit: Photoshop / Illustrator / Sketch / Axure / Fireworks / Balsamiq
