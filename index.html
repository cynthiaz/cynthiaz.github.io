A BIT ABOUT ME.

I studied Industrial Design and minored in Psychology at Carleton University. I'm currently a UX/UI designer at IBM, Toronto.

My design spirit is kept alive by the desire to become an intimate part of how things interact with human communities. To me, great designs are enablers of lifestyles: they fit into the invisible gaps in our lives and establish delightful and sustainable relationships with people. I believe design isn't just about ideas or the moment of Eureka. It's a process of creative problem solving: to observe, to think, to practice; learning the why and learning to foresee.

Feel free to peek around here...and don't be a stranger!
