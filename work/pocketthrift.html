POCKET THRIFT

Marketplace made for students

A mobile marketplace app designed for college and university students, who often need to buy or sell secondhand stuff. With Pocket Thrift, students are instantly connected to the student community around them and are able to find offers that closely match their needs. It offers a friendly marketplace for each school and makes buying and selling simple and accessible.

Interaction Design, Mobile Interface

Brief

Students studying away from home often require temporary residence, furniture, appliances and study material, many of which can be acquired from others at more efficient prices to accommodate the budget of a student. However, very few platforms cater to students by providing a marketlplace that is specific to their needs and visually informative.

The design objective of this app is to provide a centralized virtual marketplace that targets student communities, to help students in buying and selling secondhand items from each other effectively.

This is an individual project completed in four days, from ideation, wireframing to the final prototype.

User Flow & Sketches

Wireframes

Wireframes were created after the concept and user flow were consolidated. They were used to test and refine the navigation and the content layout; as well as evaluating the usability and the clarification of features.

UI screens

Selecting your community
Setting up requires very little effort on the user's side. The initial onboarding process is kept simple by selecting the school which the user attends. Signup is not required at the moment, and users are able to immediately start browsing offers within the selected community.

Discover
This is the screen users will be brought to after selecting their community. It lists all the items that are currently being offered by others. A quick filter is provided at the top based on 3 criteria: time, price and distance. You can also choose a specific category by clicking on the icon at the top right corner.

My Stuff
My stuff is where you keep track of things that matter to you: your listings, bookmarked items, and private messages. You can start selling anything that you no longer need here. It is made simple into 3 steps: 1. Take a picture 2. Fill in a bit of information about the item. 3. Publish. All steps are kept on the same screen and only imporant information is required to ensure the ease of creating a post.

Your Profile
The user's account is linked to the school's email as a verification of identity. Within the app, everyone has a public profile that shares information the user chooses to disclose about themselves.
